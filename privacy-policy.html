CanIEat Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

CanIEat ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Food Search Queries: When you search for food safety information, we process your search terms to provide relevant guidance.
  • App Usage: We collect information about how you interact with our app to improve our services.

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: We collect information about your app usage, including features used and time spent in the app.
  • Device Information: We collect device type, operating system version, and app version for technical support.
  • Crash Reports: We collect crash reports and performance data to improve app stability.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide food safety guidance and recommendations
  • Improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • Analyze app usage to enhance our services
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud

4. Data Storage and Security

Important: We prioritize your privacy. Your food search queries are processed securely and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • Your search queries are processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: We use OpenAI's services to provide AI-powered food safety guidance. Your search queries are sent to OpenAI for processing but are not stored by them.
  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and subscription management

6. Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription:

  • Payment information is processed by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not store your payment information
  • Subscription status is managed through your Apple ID

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection (though this may limit app functionality)
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for adults (pregnant women and their partners). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
